Feature: DS-120 — Dashboard card navigation regression

  Bug: Calendar, Validation, and AI Assist dashboard cards do not navigate
  when clicked. Linked story DS-119 defines expected navigation targets.

  # Regression coverage (DS-119 acceptance criteria)

  Scenario: Programs dashboard card navigates to Programs page
    Given I am logged in as admin
    And I am on the Dashboard
    When I click the Programs dashboard card
    Then I navigate to /programs

  Scenario: Calendar dashboard card navigates to Calendar page
    Given I am logged in as admin
    And I am on the Dashboard
    When I click the Calendar dashboard card
    Then I navigate to /calendar

  Scenario: Validation dashboard card navigates to Validation page
    Given I am logged in as admin
    And I am on the Dashboard
    When I click the Validation dashboard card
    Then I navigate to /validation

  Scenario: AI Assist dashboard card navigates to AI Assist page
    Given I am logged in as admin
    And I am on the Dashboard
    When I click the AI Assist dashboard card
    Then I navigate to /cli

  Scenario: Dashboard displays expected navigation cards
    Given I am logged in as admin
    When I navigate to the Dashboard
    Then I see dashboard cards for Programs, Calendar, Validation, and AI Assist

# Notes:
# - DS-120 documents a regression where Calendar, Validation, and AI Assist
#   card clicks left the user on /. Programs card navigation was unaffected.
# - Expected routes per DS-119: /programs, /calendar, /validation, /cli.
# - Card click targets the dashboard stat card (Paper), not the sidebar nav.
